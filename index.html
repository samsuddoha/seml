Welcome to SEML Research Group

Software Engineering and Machine Learning (SEML) Research Group is a research and development team of Computer Science and Engineering department at University of Barishal . This research group was founded in 2019 with a goal of contributing in Software Engineering and Machine Learning research community individually and combinely.
