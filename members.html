Md. Samsuddoha

Assistant Professor

Department of Computer Science & Engineering

University of Barishal, Bangladesh.

msamsuddoha@bu.ac.bd

Head of Research Group

Md. Samsuddoha is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering,University of Barishal,Barishal-8254, Bangladesh . He has several years of experience in both academia and industry having previously worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh Ltd . At Samsung, he was involved in developing software solutions for various products and fixing bugs for new releases as well. He also worked as a Web Developer at Graphic People Ltd. where he was involved in several projects. He trained many students in the web development track under the LICT Project which was funded by the Ministry of ICT, Bangladesh.
In his academic role at the University of Barishal, Md. Samsuddoha is responsible for teaching computer science and engineering courses, conducting research, and supervising undergraduate and graduate students. He has a strong academic background in the field of Software Engineering having M.Sc. & B.Sc. In Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh . His research interest focuses on Software Engineering, Algorithm Analysis, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing. He has published several research papers in reputed conferences and journals. Currently, he is working on several research projects including Autism Spectrum Disorder, Fake New Detection, and Sentiment Analysis etc.

Samsuddoha is the founder and coordinator of Software Engineering and Machine Learning (SEML) Research Group . Under his supervision, many students have completed their undergraduation and got employement in reputed software companies like Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh, Brainstation 23 Ltd, and Google. Few of them also secuerd faculty position in many prestigious universities in Bangladesh including Daffodil International University, Bangladesh. He is also continuously guiding and motivating students in competitive programming and serving as coach of a few teams including Team_Robust who are doing well in IUPC, and NCPC programming contests in Bangladesh.
