Md. Samsuddoha

Assistant Professor

Department of Computer Science & Engineering

University of Barishal, Bangladesh.

msamsuddoha@bu.ac.bd

Head of Research Group

Md. Samsuddoha is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Barishal, Barishal-8254, Bangladesh. He Pursuaded his M.Sc. & B.Sc. in Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. He is the coordinator of the Software Engineering and Machine Learning Research Group at University of Barishal. His research interest focuses on Software Engineering, Algorithm Analysis, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing
